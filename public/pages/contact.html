Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my Discord bot on SyntaxBots?
To have your Discord bot featured on SyntaxBots, please contact us through our developer email (developers@syntaxbots.com) with your bot's information. Our team will review your submission and add it to our curated collection if it meets our guidelines.
Do I need to create an account to browse bots?
No! You can browse all bot listings, view detailed information, and access invite links without creating any accounts. SyntaxBots is designed to be publicly accessible for all users.
What information do you collect from visitors?
For regular browsing, we only collect basic usage analytics and essential cookies for website functionality. Discord OAuth is only used for our administrative functions, not for regular users. See our Privacy Policy for complete details.
How do I update or remove my bot listing?
Since bot listings are managed by our team, please contact us at developers@syntaxbots.com if you need to update or remove your bot's information. We'll process your request promptly.
Is SyntaxBots affiliated with Discord?
No, SyntaxBots is an independent platform for showcasing Discord bots. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Discord Inc.
How can I report inappropriate content or bots?
If you encounter inappropriate content or bots that violate our guidelines, please contact us immediately at support@syntaxbots.com with details about the issue.
